Allposters.com

Allposters.com had 20+ domains and required frequent updates from changing the homepage hero to integrating responsive web design from legacy code. As an international company, we worked with our marketing and localization teams to ensure the right content was communicated from Germany to Japan. Some countries had specific guidelines when promoting, so it was important that I was familiar with those standards when producing and/or QAing code.

Maintaining these sites required a lot of attention to detail, especially when coding pages in foreign languages. One site change translated uniformaly throughout all the sites and with images, required image optimization using srcset and size compression. I'd schedule code through Hippo, our open source cms, and when multiple pushes were required at a timeframe, i'd use JavaScript snippets to schedule pushes through the holidays and weekends. When the email team required a different module aimed towards just email users, i'd use a jQuery snippet to allow that possiblity.

One of the biggest projects was closing all the international sites down. With only a day's notice to remove this content before the holidays, this required working efficiently alongside our Product Manager and engineers. I kept certain policies and terms and conditions for existing consumers aborad, but ultimately closed the international doors for our international Allposters domains.
