Workspace Art

Entering a company in the midst of change came exciting oppotunities and a new department with a lot of financial potential called Workspace Art also known as B2B. It was a quick turnaround creating a fully functioning e-commerce site for a department with new leadership. With the help of the design team, marketing and our engineers, we launched the site within a matter of months, increaing profits for the company just on its first day.

Building another domain from scratch gave us the opprtunity to use modern coding tools such as Flexbox CSS and a JavaScript library called Flickity for touch, responsive carousels. Learning how to master these skills on the spot gave me and our team a stronger collection to build from as the company progressed.

Other than creating modules on the intial mockup of the homepage, I was also tasked with creating the first Photos to Art landing page to help businesses customize their art. This involved working side-by-side with a visual designer for high quality results as well as building on JavaScript code for better performance.
