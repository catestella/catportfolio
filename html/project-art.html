Art.com

Art.com is the golden child of the company with frequent remodels and updates ranging from promotional banner offers to creating a new look and feel for the French domain. Within my first months there, I was in charge of making our homepage full-width and therefore worked with the management team and designers on how to progress on full-bleed mobile-first responsive designs, thus laying the foundation for the rest of our Art domains. In keeping with standards, we also ensured our developments had cross-brower funtionality across devices and computers both PC and Mac using Microsoft Remote Desktop.

As the first site to utilize a headless CMS, I used code standards within the team to take adavantage of our React templates. I've also worked alongside our Senior Engineer to push deployments after a series of reviews committed on GitHub.

Constantly working on improvement, I worked closely with our Senior Web Analytics Developer to perform A/B tests using the Optimizely platform. I also frequently got requests from the head of the SEM team to create modules in an effort to increase visibility in search engine results. Lastly, I was no stranger to problem solving and debugging. When an issue arose on PagerDuty or Slack, I'd aim to reproduce the issue in order to solve it or worked closely with my team on finding an efficent solution.
