Marketing Campaigns

As an e-commerce company, marketing campaigns were crucial to the business in an effort to gain more profit during holidays and special events. Leading the marketing front for the team, I used my time management skills and worked between the marketing team and designers to deliver the modules efficiently and thoroughly under tight deadlines.

This resulted in creating responsive landing pagesstrong> for Valentine's Day, introducing CSS Grid to the homepage hero to capture more offers, and the launch of Christmas gift guides and homepage remodeling. For the holidays, this involved coding material weeks in advance, building contingency plans and being on PagerDuty when bugs needed to be fixed live.

In my first year, I introduced parallax scrolling features for Black Friday to entice our consumers to shop and last Halloween, I utilized Keyframes to make the offer boxes float to capture the season. Google Analytics tracking was also a necessity to track conversion during high season, so we had it as a vital asset to our Quality Assurance process amongst developers.
