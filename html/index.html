About

I'm not your average Cat.
Originally born and raised in San Francisco, I have been all over the world pursuing opportunities and self-growth. Studying abroad, I have snorkeled with Nemo in the Great Barrier Reef, got an IMDB page playing a zombie in an award-winning short film in Los Angeles and in China, lifted an English school department with stronger curriculum and creativity that helped reinvent it to become what it is today.

Returning after a few years abroad in Asia, I joined Codify Academy as a student then later as a mentor. Helping students understand the fundamentals helped me take the leap into the world of tech as a Jr. Front End Web Developer for Art.com. With a few beers at-hand, I laid the foundation for our full-width responsive designs and helped launch our site rebrand. I got promoted as a Web Developer and helped on-board and train our newest team members with end-to-end development.

So, how can I help you today?
